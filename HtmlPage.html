RyanAir Hand Luggage Allowance

With the recent changes introduced in RyanAir Baggage policies, things have gotten much easier. Since RyanAir is a low-cost airline, their baggage policy is quite simple to understand. Let us explain you as to what baggage allowance are you eligible to after booking RyanAir flights

  • You are allowed to take 55x40x20cm (dimensions) larger cabin bag; while 35x20x20cm smaller cabin bag dimensions
  • A maximum of 10kg weight is allowed.
  • The larger bag will be placed in there hold and free if it meets the rules and regulations of the flight.
  • For any excess hand luggage, you will be charged 50 British Pounds per item.

Ryan Air Hold Luggage Allowance

  • You are allowed to take 81x119x 119 cm (dimensions) larger bags.
  • The maximum weight of the bag should not exceed 32 kgs.
  • Please not that Ryanair baggage allowance can be pooled between passengers on the same Ryanair flight booking.
  • The fees of the hold luggage vary depending upon the route that you are flying to.
